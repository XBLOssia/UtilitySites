WPSD# 1 Tech Tip!

If you have questions or issues with your device, the Tech Department may be able to help you!

Teachers, if you have a device that is giving you trouble, submit a helpdesk ticket at support.willistonschools.org. We also have a large number of troubleshooting articles on the support page which may answer your questions!

High School students having trouble with a device may visit us at the tech office on the first floor.
